End-to-End Software Implementation

Hazenberg IT manages software implementation projects from start to finish. Whether it’s a Greenfield project, a Brownfield upgrade, or a complex migration, Hazenberg IT ensures a structured approach. From high- and low-level design to implementation and operational support—no loose ends, no surprises, just well-thought-out solutions that work.

Customer Success & Consultancy

A successful implementation requires more than just technology. Hazenberg IT acts as a bridge between different teams, whether it's customers and product teams or internal departments within an organization. By aligning technical and business objectives, Hazenberg IT facilitates smooth implementation and collaboration. From strategic advice to hands-on guidance, Hazenberg IT supports organizations in achieving their IT goals.

DevOps, Automation & Security

Hazenberg IT helps organizations develop and manage robust and scalable applications. With expertise in backend development using Scala, Python, and Golang, stable and efficient systems are built. By applying best practices such as Infrastructure as Code (IaC) and GitOps, processes are streamlined and automated. Automation plays a central role in managing deployments, release pipelines, and Kubernetes clusters. Additionally, security is integrated into the process, with continuous monitoring and vulnerability detection to keep systems secure and up to date.
